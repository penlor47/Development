Places to stay:

There is a Premier Inn in Arnold just over the hill from the venue There are lots of independent hotels and B&B's in Sherwood, along the A60

The venues location is very close to Nottingham. Any hotel in the north of Nottingham will likely be within a 15-25min drive.

Transport:

All independent Taxi companies are linked. Family have used DG Taxis and the company that the venue uses is 200 CARS

It is a 10-25min taxi from most hotels in North Nottingham to the venue, depending on where you are staying.

There are also 2 buses that stop directly outside the Woodborough Hall, these are the 46 and 47 buses. These buses do not run till the end of the reception. However, they are an option for getting to the venue.

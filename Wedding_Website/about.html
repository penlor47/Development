Places to stay:

Transport:

All independent Taxi companies are linked. Family have used DG Taxis and the company that the venue uses is 200 CARS

It is a 10-25min taxi from most hotels in North Nottingham to the venue, depending on where you are staying.

There is 1 bus that stops not far from Woodborough Hall, this is the 61 bus. This bus does not run till the end of the reception. However, it is an option for getting to the venue.
